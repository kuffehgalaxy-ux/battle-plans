# Wall of Pain

The purpose of Battle Plans is to eliminate or reduce the following officer frustrations.

## Territory Battles

- Officers constantly switch between SWGOH, Discord, spreadsheets and bots.
- It is difficult to see who has not acted.
- Officers manually check every planet.
- There is no comparison between the battle plan and reality.
- ROTE only shows earned stars, not progress towards the next one.
- Officers repeatedly send manual reminder messages.
- There is no central command dashboard.

## Desired Outcomes

Battle Plans should become the first application officers open during Territory Battles.

Officers should understand the current situation in under 30 seconds.

Battle Plans should recommend the next action whenever possible.
