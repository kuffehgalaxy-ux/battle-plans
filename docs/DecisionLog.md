# Decision Log

This document records major product and technical decisions made throughout the development of Battle Plans.

---

## D001

Focus initial development on Territory Battles.

Reason:
Territory Battles are the most officer-intensive guild event and provide the greatest opportunity to reduce officer workload.

---

## D002

Battle Plans will be mobile-first.

Reason:
Most officers monitor guild progress from their phones.

---

## D003

The Command Centre is the application's primary screen.

Reason:
Every officer should understand the current guild situation within seconds of opening the application.

---

## D004

Battle Plans should recommend actions rather than simply display data.

Reason:
The application is an officer assistant, not just a tracker.

---

## D005

Discord will be the primary communication platform.

Reason:
Most guilds already use Discord for coordination.

---

## D006

Every screen follows the same hierarchy.

Status

↓

Attention

↓

Recommended Action

↓

Details

Reason:
Consistency reduces cognitive load during live events.
