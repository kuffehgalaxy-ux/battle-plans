# Design Principles

Battle Plans is designed around one simple objective:

Reduce officer workload.

Every screen should answer four questions.

## 1. What is happening?

Provide an immediate overview.

---

## 2. What needs attention?

Highlight exceptions, not everything.

---

## 3. What should happen next?

Recommend a clear action.

---

## 4. Who needs to act?

Show the relevant members.

---

## Design Rules

- Information before decoration.
- Mobile first.
- Fewer clicks than SWGOH.
- Progressive disclosure.
- Every button should save officers time.
- Every notification should have a purpose.
