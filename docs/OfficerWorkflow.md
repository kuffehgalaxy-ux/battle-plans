# Officer Workflow

This document describes how Territory Battles are managed today and how Battle Plans will simplify the process.

---

## Current Workflow

### Before Phase

Officer prepares Discord strategy.

Includes:

- Planet targets
- Star targets
- Preloads
- Priorities
- Notes

Posts manually.

---

### During Phase

Officer repeatedly checks:

- GP remaining
- Star progress
- Combat completion
- Deployment completion

Updates Discord when objectives are reached.

---

### End of Phase

Officer prepares next phase strategy.

Posts manually.

---

## Pain Points

- Repetitive Discord messages
- Constant checking of GP
- Manual reminders
- Manual target changes
- Multiple tools required

---

## Battle Plans Workflow

Officer creates Battle Plan.

↓

Battle Plans generates Discord Orders.

↓

Officer publishes.

↓

Battle Plans monitors progress.

↓

Officer receives recommendations.

↓

Officer publishes updates.

↓

Next phase created automatically.
