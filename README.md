Battle Plans

«Plan. Publish. Command.»

Battle Plans is an open-source officer command platform for Star Wars: Galaxy of Heroes.

Designed by officers, for officers, Battle Plans helps guild leadership plan, track and execute Territory Battles without relying on multiple spreadsheets, Discord messages and manual tracking.

---

Mission

Battle Plans exists to make Territory Battle management simple.

Our goal is that an officer can understand the current state of a Territory Battle in 30 seconds or less, then immediately know what action needs to be taken.

Battle Plans does not replace officer decision making.

It supports officers by reducing repetitive tasks and providing a single operational view of the guild.

Current Features (Version 0.1 Roadmap)

Command Centre

A live overview of the guild.

- Active events
- Territory Battle status
- Officer alerts
- Quick navigation

---

Battle Plans

Create and manage Territory Battle strategy.

- Planet objectives
- Star targets
- Preloads
- Officer notes
- Publish directly to Discord

---

Territory Battle Dashboard

A tactical view of the current phase.

- Planet progress
- GP remaining
- Combat completion
- Deployment completion
- Recommended actions

---

Action Board

The operational heart of Battle Plans.

Automatically identifies members who:

- Have not acted
- Have completed combat only
- Have deployed only
- Have completed the phase

Includes one-click reminders and officer reminder history.

---

Publish Orders

Generate Discord-ready Territory Battle orders directly from the current Battle Plan.

---

Phase Reports

Quickly review phase progress without opening the game.

Philosophy

Battle Plans is built around one simple promise.

«Every feature must solve a real officer problem observed during a live Territory Battle.»

If a feature doesn't save officer time, reduce clicks or improve clarity, it probably doesn't belong.
